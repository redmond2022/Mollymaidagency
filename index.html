You Might Ask

Frequently Asked Questions

1 Do you offer any discounts or promotions?
Yes! We regularly offer discounts for first-time clients, recurring cleaning packages, and seasonal promotions. Be sure to check our website or sign up for our newsletter to stay updated on our latest deals and offers.
2 Are your cleaning staff insured and background-checked?
Yes, all of our cleaning professionals are fully insured and background-checked for your peace of mind. We ensure that our team is trustworthy and reliable, so you can feel confident inviting them into your home or business.
3 Can I request special cleaning tasks?
Absolutely! We offer customizable cleaning services, so if you have specific requests (such as deep cleaning appliances, organizing, or cleaning hard-to-reach areas), just let us know when booking, and we’ll do our best to accommodate your needs.
4 Do you provide cleaning supplies, or do I need to provide them?
We bring all of our own cleaning supplies and equipment. Our staff is equipped with the tools needed to complete the job, so you don’t need to worry about anything.
5 Do I need to be home for the cleaning service?
No, you do not need to be home during the cleaning service, although you’re welcome to be. Many of our clients prefer to give us access to their property through a lockbox, key, or security code. We also offer flexible cleaning hours to work around your schedule.
